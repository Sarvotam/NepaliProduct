<h1>Trademark/ Brand protection in Nepal </h1>
<div class="text home-text">
<div class="row">
<div class="col-md-3 col-sm-3">
<p>Nepal, Trademark protection can be obtained through registration. You are well advised to register the trademark in order to obtained stronger protection, particularly in case of conflict with an identical or confusingly similar trademark. In general, any person either national or foreigner who intends to use a trademark can apply for registration. It can be either an individual or a legal entity. The registration of trademark is not compulsory; however, it is highly advisable, as registration of trademark provides exclusive rights to prevent unauthorized use of the trademark. Owner of trademark/ applicant can register the trademark in Nepal with national route hiring by local lawyers or attorneys or agents.</p>
